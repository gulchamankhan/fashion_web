ABOUT US

 — Comfort and Quality Come First.

Johanna Innsbruck and Linda Copperfield have always dreamed of comfortable women's clothing that would look appropriate in any circumstances.

This is how the Jolly House brand appeared – it is a brand for women who like to feel confident, seductive, and stylish in any situation. We use only natural fabrics and pay great attention to details that make the difference.

WHY CHOOSE US

 — Things You'll Love About Jolly House.

We create each outfit for you with great love and care.

— Jolly brand is:
Capsule collections you can wear on any occasion;
Only high quality and eco-friendly materials;
A great variety of outfits for every taste and size.

We know the main problem of all women – a lot of clothes in the wardrobe but nothing to wear. Therefore, we develop capsule collections where clothes go well with each other. You can mix them as well to create stunning new looks!
